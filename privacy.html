LinkNest Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

LinkNest is a neighborhood book-sharing app: you list books you own, discover books your neighbors are sharing, and arrange to borrow and lend them. This policy explains what information LinkNest collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it short and in plain language, because that is how we would want to read it ourselves.

The short version: we collect only what the app needs to work — your account details, the neighborhood location you set, and the content you create (books, borrow requests, messages, reviews). Other users never see your exact location. We do not run ads, we do not use third-party analytics or trackers, and we never sell your data.

1. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and password. Used to create and sign in to your account. Passwords are stored only in hashed form by our authentication provider; we never see them.
  • Display name and profile photo (optional). Shown to other users alongside your books, messages, and reviews.

Location

  • During setup you place a pin for your home neighborhood and choose a discovery radius. This is how the app shows you books near you and shows your books to nearby neighbors.
  • If you grant the location permission, your device location is used only to help you place that pin. LinkNest never tracks your location in the background.
  • Other users never see your exact coordinates. Books on the map are shown at a randomly offset position (up to about 50 meters away from the true point), and your stored location is protected on the server so it can never be read by other users — only the approximate, offset position is ever shared.

Content you create

  • Book listings (title, author, category, condition, cover photo, visibility setting).
  • Borrow requests and borrow history (request, approval, return dates, cancellations).
  • Chat messages — each conversation is scoped to a specific borrow between you and one other user.
  • Reviews and tags you leave after a borrow.
  • Clubs you create or join, friendships, block lists, and reports you submit.

Notifications

  • If you enable push notifications, we store a push token issued for your device and your notification preferences, so we can notify you about borrow requests, messages, friend requests, and due-date reminders. You can turn these off at any time in the app or in your device settings.

What we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers, no third-party analytics, no trackers.
  • No contact list or address book access.
  • No background or continuous location tracking.

2. How we use your information

  • To run the service: showing nearby books, handling borrows and returns, delivering messages and notifications, and computing community stats such as your on-time-return rate and reputation.
  • To keep the community safe: handling reports, enforcing blocks, and limiting abusive behavior.
  • Nothing else. We do not use your data for advertising, we do not profile you, and we never sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. What other users can see

  • Your display name, profile photo, and community stats (books shared, on-time-return rate, reputation).
  • Your book listings, according to the visibility you choose per book — Anyone nearby, Friends only, or Private (visible only to you).
  • The approximate (offset) position of your listed books on the map — never your exact location.
  • Reviews you write, and messages you send to the specific person you are borrowing from or lending to.

4. Service providers

LinkNest stores data with a small number of providers that act on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is hosted on Amazon Web Services in Canada (ca-central-1 region).
  • Expo / Apple / Google — push notification delivery. Your push token passes through Expo's push service and Apple (APNs) or Google (FCM) to reach your device.

These providers process data only to provide their service to us. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • We keep your data for as long as your account is active.
  • You can delete your account at any time inside the app (Profile → Delete account). This permanently removes your account, profile, books, borrow history, messages, reviews, friendships, push tokens, and uploaded photos. Deletion cannot be undone.
  • If you cannot access the app, email us (address below) and we will delete your account for you.

6. Security

All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (TLS). Access to data on the server is restricted with row-level security rules, so each user can only read and write what the app's sharing rules allow. Your exact location is additionally locked down so that it is never readable by other users, even in approximate queries.

7. Children

LinkNest is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

You can view and update your profile, location, books, and notification preferences directly in the app, and delete your account as described above. Depending on where you live (including Canada and US states with privacy laws), you may also have rights to request access to or deletion of your personal information — email us and we will honor those requests.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and notify you in the app before the changes take effect.

10. Contact
